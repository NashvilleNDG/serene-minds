Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

SereneMind Therapy ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our services.

Information We Collect

We may collect information you provide directly, such as name, email, phone number, and message content when you use our contact form, request an appointment, or communicate with us. We also collect information automatically when you visit our website (e.g., browser type, IP address, pages visited) through standard technologies such as cookies or analytics, if applicable.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to respond to inquiries, schedule appointments, provide therapy services, send administrative or practice-related communications, and improve our website and services. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Confidentiality & HIPAA

As a mental health practice, we are committed to protecting the confidentiality of your health information in accordance with applicable law, including the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA) where applicable. Clinical information shared in the context of therapy is governed by our professional and legal obligations.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Communication through our website contact form may be transmitted over the internet; for highly sensitive information, we encourage you to contact us by phone.

Third-Party Services

Our website may use third-party services (e.g., form submission, scheduling, analytics). These providers have their own privacy policies governing their use of your information.

Your Rights

You may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information, or to withdraw consent where applicable. Contact us at info@serenemindspsychotherapy.com or (786) 972-7110 to exercise these rights or with any privacy-related questions.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect the most recent version. Continued use of our website or services after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
