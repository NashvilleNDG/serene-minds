METHODOLOGY

Treatment Approaches

Every client is unique. I draw from multiple evidence-based approaches to create a personalized treatment plan that works for you.

Motivational Interviewing

A collaborative approach that strengthens your own motivation for change.

Cognitive Behavior Therapy (CBT)

Identify and change negative thought patterns that affect behavior and emotions.

Mindfulness & Meditation

Develop present-moment awareness to reduce stress and increase well-being.

Solution-Focused Therapy

Goal-oriented approach focusing on solutions rather than problems.

REBT

Rational Emotive Behavior Therapy to challenge irrational beliefs.
