FAQ

Frequently Asked
Questions

Everything you need to know before starting your therapy journey.

I offer individual therapy, couples and family therapy, women's programs (including single parenting, divorce, and self-esteem), teen and child therapy, and clinical supervision for interns. Sessions are available in person in Florida and online via secure telehealth throughout Florida.

Session rates vary by service type (individual, couples, family). I accept select insurance plans and also offer self-pay options. Please contact me or see the Insurance & Pricing section for current rates and accepted plans. I'm happy to provide superbills for out-of-network reimbursement.

Yes. I offer secure, HIPAA-compliant telehealth sessions to clients anywhere in Florida. You can attend from the comfort of your home using a confidential video platform. Many clients find online therapy just as effective as in-person sessions.

I work with clients on anxiety, depression, trauma, grief, relationship issues, life transitions, self-esteem, ADHD, behavioral concerns in children and teens, and family conflict. Treatment is tailored to your unique needs using evidence-based approaches.

Therapy length varies by person and goals. Some clients benefit from short-term work (a few months); others prefer longer-term support. We'll discuss what makes sense for you and check in regularly on progress. There's no one-size-fits-all timeline.

I provide therapy in English, Gujarati, and Hindi. If you or a family member are more comfortable in Gujarati or Hindi, we can conduct sessions in that language.

Reach out by phone at (786) 972-7110 or use the contact form on this site. I offer a free 15-minute consultation so we can discuss your needs and whether we're a good fit. There's no obligation — just a conversation about how I can help.
